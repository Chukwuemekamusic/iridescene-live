Will the event be recorded?

Yes — this edition is a live recording. By attending, you're part of the atmosphere captured on the record.

What time should I arrive?

Doors open at 4:00pm and the show starts at 5:00pm. Arrive early — seating for the recording is limited and cameras roll on time.

Is there a dress code?

Come comfortable and camera-ready — the room will be on film. No strict dress code.
